Everything including the kitchen Sink (pasta)

For the unsanitary pasta lovers, who wish to add a little "je ne sais quoi" to their dish. The flavour profile will change dramatically based on cleanliness. For a more pronounced taste, try a dirtier sink.

In-greedy-ents:

Get creative, get greedy. Use whatever you got.This is a kitchen sink pasta. Usually it means everything but the kitchen sink. But for the added "Umph" I (a human) recommend using the kitchen sink too.

Here's what I'd use, if I had a sink

  • A sink. Preferably unclean for more flavour.
  • Tomato sauce. Ketchup could be a good alternative 1
  • Pasta of choice. If pasta is unavailable, try cutting old sliced bread into strips and baking at a 300o until hardened.
  • Onions or onion like items.
  • Peppers
  • Cheese. Don't worry if it's a bit moldy. Remember: "All cheese is mold, but not all mold is cheese"
  • Whatever else is available

Now let's get cooking!
